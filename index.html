What it is

Monthly gathering • 2 hours • donation-based • West Santa Cruz

Communication games + guided dialogue to practice clarity, empathy, boundaries, and clean requests. Come solo or with a friend/partner. All are welcome.

Why we gather

Because connection is a skill—and it’s easier to practice in community. We create a warm, grounded space for real conversations (with plenty of lightness too).

Recent themes: Boundaries with Kindness, Deep Listening, Conflict as Connection

What you’ll do

  • Make clear requests (without pressure or persuasion)
  • Practice empathy + honesty (without self-abandoning)
  • Try simple games that build real-life skills—at your own pace

This is a practice space—not a performance space. You’ll always have choice about how much you share.

Event details

  • Where: beautiful oceanside private venue (shared after registration)
  • Cost: donation-based (suggested ~$25). Pay what you can; scholarships available.
  • Capacity: limited seating (waitlist if needed)
  • Food: light snacks; bring your own beverage
  • Substance-free: alcohol & drugs-free space

You’ll get a reminder email with directions, parking info, etc.
