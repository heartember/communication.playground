What it is

Monthly gathering • 2 hours • ~20 people • NVC-inspired

Practice real connection through games and dialogue. Come alone or with a friend or partner. All are welcome.

Why we gather

To nurture connection, authenticity, and belonging in community. We create an emotionally safe space through dialogues, improv, vibrant questions, and group wisdom.

Recent themes: Boundaries with Kindness, Deep Listening, Conflict as Connection

What you'll do

  • Practice NVC-inspired skills for clarity, empathy, and honest requests
  • Try simple communication "games" that stay kind and grounded
  • Explore playful connection and real depth (at your own pace)

This is a practice space—not a performance space. You'll always have choice about how much you share.

Event details

  • Where: beautiful oceanside private venue (shared after registration)
  • Cost: donation-based (often ~$25) supporting scholarships
  • Capacity: typically ~20 people (waitlist if needed)
  • Food: light snacks; bring your own beverage
  • Container: alcohol/substance-free

You'll get a reminder email with directions, parking info, etc.
