What it is

Communication Playground is a monthly gathering of folks who want to practice real connection— come alone or with a friend or partner. (All diversities welcome.)

Why we do it

Because connection, authenticity, communication, play, and learning create belonging in community—and a quality life.

Our intention

To nurture an emotionally safe environment through dialogues, improv, vibrant questions, experiential exercises, and mining of group wisdom.

What you’ll do

  • Practice NVC-inspired skills for clarity, empathy, and honest requests
  • Try simple communication “games” that stay kind and grounded
  • Explore both playful connection and real depth (at your pace)

This is a practice space—not a performance space. You’ll always have choice about how much you share.

Event details

  • Where: beautiful oceanside private venue (shared after registration)
  • Cost: donation-based (often ~$25) supporting scholarships
  • Capacity: typically ~20 people (waitlist if needed)
  • Food: light snacks; bring your own beverage
  • Container: alcohol/substance-free

You’ll get a reminder email with directions, parking info, etc.
